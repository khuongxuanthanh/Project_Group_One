BECOME A VOLUNTEER

Get involved

Join the team

Volunteering at Aptech is a fantastic way to get involved in a sport or industry that you are passionate about, in a role that has the main purpose of benefitting someone else – in this case, you will be protecting the safety of competitors (as well as officials, staff and other volunteers). Here is a list and description of the various volunteer roles we need at every race: Registration: 2-3 people to set riders up for the race. Staging: organizing the riders so they are ready to get on the gate when it is time for their moto to race. Finish Line: recording the riders in order as they cross the finish line
